Froggy settings
The frog scrunches and hops as you type. Uncheck to keep it still while you work.
Master switch. Uncheck to freeze every frog — yours and friends' — to a static pose.

Shout sends an all-caps message to every connected friend at once. Open it from the megaphone button on your frog.

Click the Pomodoro frog button to start a focus block — the countdown floats above the frog. When it runs out the frog hops; tap it to start your break, and tap again to focus. Click the Pomodoro button any time to stop.

Click the Countdown frog button to start the timer — it floats above the frog. When it reaches zero, your message pops up in a dialog. Click the button again to cancel.
